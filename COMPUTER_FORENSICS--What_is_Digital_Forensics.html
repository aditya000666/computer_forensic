What is Digital Forensics


• Application of Computer Science and Investigative procedures for legal purpose involving the analysis of digital evidence after proper search authority, chain of custody, validation with mathematics, use of validated tools, repeat, ability, reporting and possible expert presentation
• Digital Forensics is defined as process of preservation, identification, extraction and documentation of computer evidence which can be used by court of law
• Science of finding evidence from digital media like computer, mobile phone, server or network
• Digital Forensic encompasses much more than laptop and desktop computers
• Also includes analysis of images, videos and audio both analog and digital
