What steps are taken in Crime Scene Investigation


• Cyber crime scene is different from conventional crime scene
• Digital evidence is fragile and can be tampered easily
• The sequence of steps for Digital Crime Scene investigations are
• Identifying and Securing the crime scene
• Documentation of scene of offence
• Collection of Evidence
• Forensic Duplication
• Conducting interview
• Labelling and Documenting of evidence
• Packaging and Transportation of evidence
