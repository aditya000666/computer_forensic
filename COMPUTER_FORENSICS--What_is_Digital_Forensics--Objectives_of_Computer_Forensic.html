Objectives of Computer Forensic


• Helps to recover, analyse and preserve computer and related materials in such manner that it helps investigation agency to present them as evidence in court of law
• Postulate the motive behind the crime and identity of main culprit
• Design procedures at suspected crime scene which helps to ensure that digital evidence obtain is corrupted
• Data acquisition and duplication involves recovering deleted file and deleted partitions from digital media to extract evidence and validate them
• Helps to identify evidence quickly and allows to estimate the potential impact of malicious activity on victim
• Preserve the evidence by following chain of custody
